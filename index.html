ABOUT US

GENESIS

Every year, ISA-RAIT holds it's own technical festival GENESIS, which is widely popular among the college students and is much awaited one. Our committee is known for coming up with innovative ideas for the events in the festival, and there are always some new attractions to look out for. Being the highlight of the Odd Semester, ISA-RAIT always tries to inculcate various cognitive and soft skill within the students through the medium of GENESIS.

PRAKALP

A mini project competition is a platform for many students to showcase their projects. Not only RAIT, but various other college students also take part every year. Many innovative projects are displayed and participants put forward their best work in front of the judges with enthusiasm and confidence. This competition always proves to be extremely successful in encouraging and motivating students to think, develop and build.

WORKSHOPS

ISA-RAIT aims to bridge the gap between the students and the Industry by developing technical knowledge of the students. Our Technical team conducts workshops and arrange seminars to develop the technical and other required skills of the students to make them industry-ready.

NON-CORE TEAMS

Technical Team

Technical team lead the technical workshops, seminars and expert talks conducted for the students and also train them in the technical realm as per their needs.

Creative Team

Creative team takes charge of the artistic responsibilities of all the events and is a forerunner in determining the festival's idea and artistic layout for all activities.

Editorial Team

Editorial team is in charge of publishing the annual Newsletter (Automatrix) and Magazine (ISAAC) of ISA and also contribute to the creatives put up on the social media handle of ISA.

Administrative Team

Administrative Team primarily looks into writing permission letters and other clerical work and also handles the social media accounts of ISA RAIT over various platforms.

Public Relation Team

Public Relations team is a bridge that connects ISA RAIT to the world outside the committee through various media campaigns and helps the committee to maintain a positive image.

Sponsership Team

Sponsership team takes charge of bringing the best of sponsorships which proves to be helpful for all the annual events, including our very own Techfest (Genesis).

Membership Team

Membership team assist the students through the entire process, starting from getting the ISA membership so that students can enjoy the perks of ISA membership.

Logistics Team

Logistics team is responsible for the comprehensive planning and execution of events, as well as the acquisition, transportation and storage of materials.
